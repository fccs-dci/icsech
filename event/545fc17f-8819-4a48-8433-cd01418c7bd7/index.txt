<p style="line-height:1.2"><span style="font-size:12pt; font-variant:normal; white-space:pre-wrap"><span style="font-family:Garamond,serif"><span style="color:#000000"><span style="font-weight:400"><span style="font-style:normal"><span style="text-decoration:none">read specific pan-Asian texts to reveal their internal logic as it relates to larger historical contexts. Looking through the lens of pan-Asianist thought we see different images of China, throughout the socialist and postsocialist periods. These different images shed light on major problems in Asia today, including the problem of capitalist domination, the legacy of Hegel and Eurocentrism, the construction of Asian unity, and finally the issue of war memory in Asia.&nbsp;</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
