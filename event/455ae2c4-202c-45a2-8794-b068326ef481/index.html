FOREIGN INVESTMENTS AND SECURITY: A NORDIC PERSPECTIVE ON CHINA’S RISE - CHERN CHINA-EUROPE RESEARCH HIGHLIGHTS

Starting Time 活動開始時間

April 8, 2021, 3:00 PM UTC

(In your time zone. 閣下所在時區）

Participants 嘉賓

Morten S. Andersen (Norwegian Institute of International Affairs - NUPI), Hege Medin (Norwegian Institute of International Affairs - NUPI), Ulf Sverdrup (Norwegian Institute of International Affairs - NUPI)

Organizers 主協辦機構

China in Europe Research Network, Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam (Chair)

Mode 活動形式
Online
Langauges 語言
English
Description 詳情

Looking at Chinese Investments in Northern Europe in a presentation of the COINS (Consequences of Foreign Investments for National Security) project, Morten S. Andersen, Hege Medin and Ulf Sverdrup, all from the Norwegian Institute of International Affairs (NUPI), will present results from a comprehensive statistical mapping of Chinese investments, a survey on attitudes towards foreign investments, and discuss some of the challenges facing governments when designing effective screening mechanisms. Questions will be welcome throughout the event via chat and the floor will be opened for a discussion with the audience in the final part of the event.
