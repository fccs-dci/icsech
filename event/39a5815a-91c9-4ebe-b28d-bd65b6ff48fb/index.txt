<p><strong>Max Lu</strong></p>

<p>Ph.D. student in Education at Harvard University and Co-founder of Dialogues on Asian Universities (DAU)</p>

<p>Max Lu co-founded the Dialogue on Asian Universities (DAU) in 2020 alongside Da Hsuan Feng, Tan Eng Chye, and Tony F Chan. DAU is a premier platform for university leaders to explore the challenges and opportunities in Asian higher education. Max’s research focuses on the intersection of technology, media, and learning sciences, with a particular interest in utilizing Humanistic Intelligence (HI) principles to enhance learning efficiency through technology. Prior to Harvard, Max worked on news innovations at Bloomberg LP and the Los Angeles Times. He holds a Master’s and a Bachelor’s degree in engineering from the University of Toronto. Max is also an adjunct professor at the Hainan University Belt and Road Research Institute and a technology fellow at the USC Annenberg Center on Communication Leadership and Policy.</p>
