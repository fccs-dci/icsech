The Chinese Business History research cluster at the Hong Kong Institute for the Humanities and Social Sciences is pleased to invite you to the following workshop. The workshop will be held in hybrid mode, so you are welcome to join us in person or via Zoom.

Register now: https://forms.gle/MxNSQa6J9yegQqCd6
