15:T41a,<p>The 13th All India Conference of China Studies (AICCS) will be organised virtually by Institute of Chinese Studies (ICS), Delhi &amp; Indian Institute of Technology, Madras (IIT-M) China Studies Centre from 28 to 30 Jan 2021. Dr. S. Jaishankar, Minister for External Affairs, will deliver the Keynote Address during the Inaugural Session on 28 Jan.</p>
<p>The conference will feature young &amp; established China scholars from more than 30 institutions across India &amp; abroad. This year’s Conference will focus on the special theme “China at the Crossroads: New Directions in Politics, Environment and Economy” in 11 sessions spread over 3 days. The best original paper presented at the AICCS by a young scholar is awarded the Mira Sinha-Bhattacharjea Award – instituted in memory of one of India&#039;s finest China scholars.</p>
<p>ICS is one of India&#039;s oldest multi-disciplinary research institutions on China &amp; E. Asia. Established in 2011, IIT Madras China Studies Centre hosts China scholars in multiple disciplines</p>
